About Me

Athena is a Behavioral Neuroscience major with a minor in Computer Science. Before attending Harvard, she went to Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School as a Visual Arts major. Since studying visual arts in highschool, Athena has been involved in design for many years. After taking a course in UX/UI, she has extended this passion to products and interfaces.

In her free time she enjoys dancing, watching Ted Talks, reading Neuroscience books, and spending time with her amazing boyfriend Kian. She is President of HSMBB.
